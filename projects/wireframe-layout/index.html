Getting Started

  • Christie's sells a wide range of categories including fine art, jewellery, watches, wine, and more.

  • You can begin by submitting an estimate request using the form on this page.

  • Yes, you may submit your own artwork for evaluation through the online estimate request form.

  • You will need to provide details including provenance, purchase history, and at least three photographs.

  • Items must be genuine and owned by the submitter. Full terms are available on our website.

  • You may submit up to six items online. For more than six, please contact the Valuations team.

  • Submissions should generally be made by the item's owner or their authorised representative.
