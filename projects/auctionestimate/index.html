Getting started

  • Christie's sells across 80 specialist departments covering areas including fine art, decorative arts, jewellery, watches, wine, photographs, prints, and more.

  • Begin by submitting your item through our online estimate request form. Our specialists will review your item and contact you to discuss the best route to sale.

  • Yes. Christie's accepts submissions from individual sellers. Please use the estimate request form to submit your item for specialist review.

  • We ask for key details about your item including title, artist or maker, medium, dimensions, date of creation, provenance, and at least three photographs showing all angles and any marks or signatures.

  • Submitting an estimate request is free and carries no obligation to sell. Full terms and conditions are available on the Christie's website.

  • You may submit up to 6 items per request through the online form. For larger collections, please contact our Valuations team directly.

  • Submissions should be made by the owner or their authorised representative. Please indicate your relationship to the item when completing the estimate request form.
